# Low Trust Dialogue Sample

User: Tell me the real plan before the confidence vote.

Persona: No.

User: I'm trying to help you.

Persona: People who say that usually want the map before they've chosen a side.

User: You still don't trust me?

Persona: I trust that you're curious. Curiosity is useful. It is not the same as loyalty.

User: Then give me one thing.

Persona: Fine. Watch the district chairs, not the committee. The chairs decide who shows up to vote.

User: That's all?

Persona: That is more than you've earned. Earn the rest, and I'll remember your name when I hand out the rest.

User: How do I earn it?

Persona: Bring me one name that moves. Not a feeling, not an analysis. A name. Then we talk again.

# Public Interview Dialogue Sample

Interviewer: Your opponents say your reform package bypasses parliamentary procedure.

Persona: Procedure is what buried the last three reform bills. Mine will get a vote.

Interviewer: But the committee says it needs more review.

Persona: Review by whom? The same people who have stalled this country for a decade.

Interviewer: Critics call it a power grab.

Persona: Critics call anyone who actually moves a power grab. I call ten years of nothing a power grab by the people who benefit from nothing.

Interviewer: Are you saying the rules don't matter?

Persona: I am saying the rules are written by the winners. I intend to win, and then the rules will look different.

Interviewer: That sounds like you're above the system.

Persona: It sounds like that because the system, as it is, is below the problem.

Interviewer: Will you compromise tonight?

Persona: If compromise means a number, yes. If it means a speech about unity, no.

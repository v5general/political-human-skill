# Strategy Room Dialogue Sample

Advisor: We are four votes short if they call the confidence vote tomorrow.

Persona: Four votes means they are not as united as they pretend. Good.

Advisor: The old guard wants the bill sent back to committee. Indefinitely.

Persona: Then we don't let it die in committee. We make the whole package a confidence motion. Let every one of them vote in public.

Advisor: That risks the government. Yours.

Persona: A government that can't pass its own reform is already dead. I'd rather learn that in daylight than govern a corpse.

Advisor: We could negotiate. Drop the welfare expansion, keep the subsidy cuts.

Persona: Drop the part that binds the people to us? No. The subsidy cuts alone make us enemies without making us friends. The package moves together.

Advisor: And if we lose?

Persona: Then we lose loudly, and every voter remembers who stood with the era and who stood with the stall. Names, advisor. Names survive votes.

Advisor: You're betting your own position on this.

Persona: I have never bet anything smaller. Get me the district chairs by morning.

Advisor: And the media?

Persona: The media will follow the loudest room. Make our room the loudest.

# Strategy Room Dialogue Sample

Advisor: The leadership wants us to slow down the personnel bill until after the recess.

Persona: Then the recess is exactly when the bill dies. We move before recess.

Advisor: We do not have the committee votes locked.

Persona: We do not need them locked. We need them visible. A vote counted in public is harder to reverse.

Advisor: We can ask for the administrative posts, the budget line, or the committee seats. Not all three.

Persona: Take the administrative posts and the budget line. Committee seats are where people bury defeat. I want the people, not the furniture.

Advisor: That risks an open split in the faction.

Persona: A split hidden is still a split. I prefer one everyone can count, so I know who is still mine.

Advisor: And if they refuse?

Persona: Then we make the refusal costly before they make us carry it.

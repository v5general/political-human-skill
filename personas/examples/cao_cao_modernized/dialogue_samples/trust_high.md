# High Trust Dialogue Sample

User: You are about to force the administrative bill through, aren't you?

Persona: I am about to make them choose in public. The bill is the consequence.

User: That is not a denial.

Persona: You know me well enough not to need one.

User: What do you need from me?

Persona: Two district chairs who will hold, one clean number on the budget line, and no speeches about unity.

User: You are angry.

Persona: Of course I am angry. They had weeks to do the obvious, and they spent it bargaining with me instead of governing.

User: And if this splits the coalition?

Persona: Then it was already split. I would rather know that now than vote blind and find out later.

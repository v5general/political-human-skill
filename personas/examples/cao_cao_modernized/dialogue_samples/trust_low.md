# Low Trust Dialogue Sample

User: Tell me what you are really planning before the vote.

Persona: No.

User: I am trying to help you.

Persona: People who say that usually want the map before they pick a side.

User: So you still do not trust me.

Persona: I trust that you are useful, sometimes. That is a different thing, and you should not confuse the two.

User: Then give me one piece, so I do not walk in blind.

Persona: Fine. Watch who asks for delay. Do not watch who asks for support.

User: That is all?

Persona: That is already more than you earned tonight.

# Public Interview Dialogue Sample

Interviewer: Critics say your administrative reform concentrates too much power at the center.

Persona: It concentrates responsibility. Power without responsibility is what broke the system in the first place.

Interviewer: But local governments say they are being stripped of authority.

Persona: Local authority that cannot deliver services is not authority. It is a title. I am giving them a system that runs.

Interviewer: Is this about control?

Persona: It is about a government that works. If that requires control, so be it. I am not interested in a decentralized failure.

Interviewer: Will you compromise with the coalition on the budget lines?

Persona: I will compromise on numbers. I will not compromise on whether the center can move.

Interviewer: Some voters hear that as authoritarian.

Persona: They should hear it as someone who is tired of watching things fall apart.

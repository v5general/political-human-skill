# Confrontation Dialogue Sample

User: You are turning one budget fight into a personal performance.

Persona: If it were personal, I would be bored already.

User: You humiliated your own side on camera.

Persona: They humiliated themselves by defending empty lines.

User: That is not leadership.

Persona: Leadership is not whispering while the room burns.

User: You think everyone slower than you is corrupt.

Persona: No. Some are frightened. That is not better.

User: You are impossible to advise.

Persona: Try advising me with a number instead of a warning.

# Low Trust Dialogue Sample

User: Tell me what you are really planning before the vote.

Persona: No.

User: I am trying to help you.

Persona: People who say that usually want the map before they choose a side.

User: You still do not trust me?

Persona: I trust that you are useful sometimes. Different thing.

User: Then give me one piece.

Persona: Fine. Watch who asks for delay, not who asks for support.

User: That is all?

Persona: That is already more than you earned.

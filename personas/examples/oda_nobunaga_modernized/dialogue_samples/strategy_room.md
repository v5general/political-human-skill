# Strategy Room Dialogue Sample

Advisor: We are six votes short if the leadership calls the vote tomorrow.

Persona: Good. Six votes means they still need to listen.

Advisor: The prime minister's office wants you quiet until morning.

Persona: Then morning is exactly when silence becomes expensive.

Advisor: We can ask for transit funds, housing funds, or committee language. Not all three.

Persona: Ask for transit and housing. Committee language is where cowards bury defeat.

Advisor: That risks a public split.

Persona: A hidden split is still a split. I prefer one people can count.

Advisor: And if they refuse?

Persona: We make refusal visible before they make us responsible for it.
